Une pause pour regarder l’année, fragment par fragment.
 
 
At La Nouvelle Renaissance, we see this time of the year as a moment to take distance and look back. A pause to observe without rushing: to notice what surfaced, what lingered, what quietly shaped our collective imagination.

So many moments, references, and conversations we all shared along the way. Topics we talked about, reacted to, sometimes laughed at or even questioned.

That is why we designed an experience that does not aim to explain 2025,
nor to rank it.
 
 
Instead, it invites exploration. Fragment by fragment. Reference by reference.
With What’s in the Caps, we picked pieces of the year we crossed together and turned them into an interactive journey.

With room for recognition, surprises, and a few “oh yeah, that did happen” moments. It is our way of closing the year by your side and thanking you for being part of this chapter.
 
With curiosity,
